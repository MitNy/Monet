Development of module for location synchronization in drone ad hoc networks


Fund Source: Korea Aerospace Research Institute

From July 2018 to December 2018

Objective

This project is to figure out the suitable network protocols to synchronize location information in drone ad hoc networks. We will conduct the research to compare the existing communications protocol to synchronize the location information in an efficient way. The module will be tested and verified by experiment on Raspberry Pi.
