Adaptive/Cognitive Routing Protocol and UAV-Relayed Load Balancing Scheme in Tactical Ad Hoc Networks


Fund Source: Agency of Defense Development

From December 2017 to December 2019

Objective

This project is to develop a new adaptive/cognitive routing protocol for UAV based tactical ad hoc networks.

The main research topic covers 1) cognitive/adaptive routing protocol, 2) load balancing algorithm, 3) mobility model for tactical ad hoc networks, 4) traffic distribution through UAV, and 5) modeling and simulation by OPNET (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OPNET)
