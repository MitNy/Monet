Real-time Stream for Mobile Object Tracking in Wireless Multimedia Sensor Networks


Fund Source: Basic Science Research Program through the National Research Foundation of Korea

From June 2018 to May 2021

Objective

The purpose of this research is to overcome the limitation of existing mobile object tracking schemes based on scalar value through real-time stream on wireless multimedia sensor networks. To achieve this goal, (m,k)-firm stream is taken while considering constraints in wireless sensor networks and multimedia traffic property. Also, path prediction algorithm for fast mobile object is also proposed. Moreover, single object tracking algorithm is extended to cover multiple objects at the same time without causing additional complexity. Finally, simulation results will be given to prove the improved performance.
